About Our School

20+ Years of

Building Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Aryan Senior Secondary School is a premier co-educational institution located in the serene village of Dhani Bhakran, near Jhumpa Khurd, in the Bhiwani district of Haryana. Affiliated to the Haryana Board of School Education (HBSE), our school has been a beacon of quality education for over two decades.

Our motto "तमसो मा ज्योतिर्गमय" — Lead me from darkness to light — encapsulates our mission to illuminate young minds through knowledge, values, and holistic development. We blend academic rigor with character building, sports, and creative arts to nurture well-rounded individuals.

Situated approximately 22 km from Siwani and 65 km from Bhiwani, our campus provides a peaceful, green environment that is perfect for focused learning away from urban distractions.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence that develops confident, creative, and responsible citizens who contribute positively to society. We envision every student becoming a beacon of light — embodying our motto of moving from darkness to enlightenment.

Our Mission

To provide quality education accessible to every child in the region, fostering intellectual curiosity, moral values, and physical fitness. We are committed to creating an inclusive learning environment where each student discovers and develops their unique potential.

Our Values

Integrity, Discipline, Respect, Excellence, and Compassion form the pillars of our educational philosophy. We instill these values through daily practices, ensuring our students become not just successful individuals, but good human beings.
